BEGINNER SPEAKING TASK

Topic: Self Introduction

Instruction: Introduce yourself in front of the class for 30–60 seconds.

You Should Mention:

  • Your name
  • Your age
  • Your school
  • Your hobby
  • Where you live

Speaking Template

Hello, everyone.

My name is __________.

I am __________ years old.

I study at __________.

My hobby is __________.

I live in __________.

Thank you.

Example

Hello, everyone.

My name is Sarah.
I am sixteen years old.
I study at SMA Harapan Bangsa.
My hobby is reading novels and listening to music.
I live in Denpasar, Bali.

Thank you.
